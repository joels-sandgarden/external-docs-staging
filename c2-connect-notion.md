# Connect Notion

Use this page when you need Notion to supply background context for Doc Holiday. Notion adds a read-only Source so Doc Holiday can read your pages as context instead of starting work on its own. See [Sources](/c1-sources.md) for the broader model.

## Connect Notion

1. Open **Sources**, select **Add Source**, and choose **Notion**.
2. Enter a **Source Name** that helps you recognize it later.
3. Enter the **Integration Key** for the Notion integration that can read the pages you want Doc Holiday to use.
4. Select **Save Changes**.

## Verify

Open **Sources** and confirm the Notion Source shows **Healthy**.
